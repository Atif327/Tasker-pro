Introduction

Flutter Tasker Pro ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. By using our app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Account Information: Email address, name, and optional profile picture for authentication purposes
  • Task Data: Tasks, subtasks, categories, notes, and related information that you create within the app
  • Usage Data: App preferences, settings, theme choices, and usage patterns to enhance your experience
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version for compatibility and troubleshooting
  • Biometric Data: Fingerprint/face recognition data (stored locally on your device only, never transmitted to our servers)

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our task management services
  • Authenticate your account securely using Firebase Authentication
  • Sync your tasks and preferences across your devices
  • Send task reminders and notifications based on your preferences
  • Personalize your app experience with custom themes and settings
  • Display relevant advertisements (only when internet connection is available)
  • Analyze app usage to improve functionality and user experience
  • Provide customer support and respond to your inquiries

Data Storage and Security

We take your data security seriously:

  • Local Storage: All your task data is stored locally on your device using SQLite database, giving you full control
  • Firebase Services: Account credentials are securely stored using Firebase Authentication with industry-standard encryption
  • Biometric Data: Never leaves your device and is managed exclusively by your device's secure enclave
  • Data Encryption: All data transmissions between your device and our servers are encrypted using SSL/TLS protocols
  • No Cloud Storage: Your tasks remain on your device and are not stored on external servers

Third-Party Services

We integrate the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication (Google LLC): For secure user authentication and account management
  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements within the app (only when internet is available)
  • Firebase Analytics: For understanding app usage patterns and improving features
  • Firebase Crashlytics: For detecting and fixing app crashes to improve stability

Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in our app. AdMob may collect and use certain data for ad personalization and measurement. Advertisements are displayed only when you have an active internet connection.

Key points about advertising:

  • Ads are shown at natural break points and do not interrupt critical app functions

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request access to your personal data stored in our systems
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data at any time from the app settings
  • Export: Export your tasks to CSV or PDF format for backup purposes
  • Correction: Update or correct your account information and preferences
  • Opt-out: Disable biometric authentication or other optional features
  • Data Portability: Take your data with you in commonly used formats

Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as your account is active. When you delete your account through the app settings, all associated data (tasks, preferences, and account information) is permanently removed from our servers and cannot be recovered. Local data on your device is also deleted.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to remove that information.

Permissions

  • Biometric: To enable fingerprint/face unlock features (optional)
  • Camera/Storage: To add profile pictures and attach files to tasks (optional)
  • Vibration: For notification alerts
